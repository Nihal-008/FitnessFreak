Healthy Nutrition Guide

Breakfast

Start your day with a nutritious breakfast that includes a mix of whole grains, protein, and fruits. Consider options like oatmeal with berries, Greek yogurt with nuts, and a banana.

Lunch

For a healthy lunch, focus on lean proteins, vegetables, and whole grains. Try a grilled chicken salad with quinoa, spinach, tomatoes, and a vinaigrette dressing.

Dinner

Opt for a balanced dinner that includes a mix of protein, vegetables, and complex carbohydrates. Consider baked salmon with sweet potatoes and steamed broccoli.

Snacks

Choose healthy snacks between meals to keep your energy levels stable. Examples include fresh fruit, nuts, yogurt, or whole-grain crackers with hummus.
